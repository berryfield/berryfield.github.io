ABOUT ME

Looking for a recording or mixing engineer for your project?

My goal as a sound engineer is to realise Your creative vision and to transparently translate it onto the recording medium. I offer bespoke recording, mixing and audio editing services to fulfil the audio needs of Your project. Together we will construct a plan for carrying out the project successfully and to make the process for You as smooth and comfortable as possible. Leave the technical execution to me so you can freely focus on the most important aspect; Your music.

My expertise is working with acoustic and electroacoustic music. See above for examples of some of my recent work. If you have any questions, would like to inquire for an offer or get more information about pricing, please don’t hesitate to contact me with the form below.
